Duncan Bradley

I am a Master’s student on the MRes Psychology course at the University of Manchester. In September 2020, I will be starting my PhD at Manchester. My research explores how we look at and understand data visualisations. I am particularly interested in depictions of raw data, and how people mentally represent graphs. As a founder member of the University of Manchester’s Open Research Working Group, I am passionate about open science and reproducibility.
